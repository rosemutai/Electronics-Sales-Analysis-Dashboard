# Electronics-Sales-Analysis-Dashboard 

## Overview
This project presents a Power Bi Dashboard analyzing sales performance for an electronic business.
The goal was to uncover insights for product performance, profitability and customer trends.

## Objectives
- Evaluate overall sales and profits
- Identify top performing products
- Analyze profit margins
- Understand customer and regional sales distribution
- Build an interactive dashboard

## Dataset Description
  The electronics dataset contains transactional sales data with:
  - Product Name
  - Product Category
  - Sales Amount
  - Quanity
  - Sales Rep
  - Shipping Cost
  - Profit
  - Currency Name
  - Customer Age
  - Customer Age
  - Customer Email
  - Percentage Discount
  - Currency
 
## Data Cleaning
For this dataset I:
    - Cleaned missing and inconsistent values
    - Converted all currencies to USD to maintain consistency
    - Created a Date Table for time based analysis
    - Created a facts and dimensional tables then established relationships between tables

## Key Metrics
- Total Sales in USD
- Tota Profit in USD
- Profit Margin (%)
- Average Profit
- Average Shipping Cost

## Dashboard Features
- KPI Cards showing total sales, total profits, total orders and Profit Margin
- Sales trends
- Profit margin by product and month
- Total Sales by product and product category
- Slicers
- Distribution of sales in differnt cities

## Key Insights
- Revenue collected was high with moderate profits, the total sales made was ~98k USD with the profits being ~12k USD.
- Profit margin was ~12%, hence moderate.
- High value products, the computers category, drive most revenue.
- However not all all high selling products have high profit margins.
- The average shipping cost, ~21 USD constributes to reduced margins

## Business Recommendations
- Review pricing strategy for low margin products and reduce huge discounts to improve profit margins.
- Focus on high performing products by promoting products with high sales.
- Optimize costs by analyzing and reducing shipping expenses.
- Expand dataset to include longer time periods to enable better growth analysis.

  ### Tools and Skills Used
  - Power BI- Data Modeling and Visualization
  - DAX- Measures and Calculations
  - Data Cleaning and Transformation
  - Business Analysis

  ## Conclusion
  This dashboard provides a clear view of sales performance and profits. The revenue is high, but improving profit margins and optimizing costs will be key to increasing the overall business performance.
